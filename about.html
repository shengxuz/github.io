About Us

Shermoss Holdings Limited is an international energy holding company committed to becoming a trusted partner and forward-looking investor in the global energy value chain.

Core Businesses

  • Energy Trading: Procurement, logistics optimization, risk management, and sales of petroleum products (especially middle distillates such as diesel, heavy oil, and fuel oil) globally, serving large industrial projects, mining, and infrastructure.
  • New Energy Investment: Actively identifying, evaluating, and investing in high-growth potential renewable energy projects (e.g., solar, wind, energy storage) and related technologies to drive energy transition.
